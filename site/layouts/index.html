Episode 2: Ops we did it again.

Pun intended. We asked, and asked, and management foolishly said ‘yes’. So here we are, a team of developer advocates doing a podcast. Our intention is to fill your daily commute with hours and hours of content about ‘the cloud.’ But don’t think we’re staying strictly with Google Cloud Platform. At least one episode of this podcast will be a solid hour of us saying the word “Docker” at various volumes and assorted accents. So come join us for a journey to the clouds... and beyond!

Hosts: Francesc Campoy Flores & Mark Mandel
Topics: Google Cloud Platform, Go, Docker
